Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 9, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Kuma Uptime Manager ("App") handles your data.

1. Data You Provide

You provide server URLs, API keys, and optional management credentials to connect to your Uptime Kuma server.

2. How Data Is Stored

Sensitive credentials (API keys and management credentials) are stored in macOS Keychain. Non-sensitive preferences (UI settings and polling options) are stored in local app preferences.

3. Network Activity

The App sends requests only to endpoints you configure (your Uptime Kuma host). The App does not include third-party analytics or ad tracking.

4. Data Sharing

The App does not sell your data and does not intentionally share your credentials with third parties.

5. Notifications

If enabled, local system notifications are used to inform you of monitor state transitions (down/recovered).

6. Data Retention

Data remains on your device until you remove credentials, reset settings, or uninstall the App.

7. Contact

For privacy questions, publish your support contact in this section before launch.

This policy template is informational and should be reviewed for legal compliance before production use.
